Loot: MS > OS +1 (mats/patterns reserved):

When you register for the raid you will choose what spec you are rolling for. If a piece of loot drops for that spec you may roll on it. If you choose to use your +1, you roll in a higher tier than those who did not use their +1. If you win the roll you lose your +1, otherwise you keep it and can use it again if more loot drops for your spec. This ensures that players will likely only get one piece of loot per raid.
